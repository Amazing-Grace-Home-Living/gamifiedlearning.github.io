THE BLACKWOOD CHRONICLE — SCROLL III

The ravens of Blackwood are no ordinary birds.

They were bound, long ago, to carry fragments of a message
too large for any one creature to hold in full.
Each raven carries a tile — a symbol, a sound, a portion of truth —
and they will not release it until the old game is played.

The game is simple: like seeks like.
Arrange the tiles so that patterns emerge,
and the ravens will sing their portion of the message.

When enough have sung, the third star blazes into view —
an emblem stitched across the night sky in fire and darkness,
visible only to those who have earned the right to look.
