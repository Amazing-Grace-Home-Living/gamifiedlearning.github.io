THE BLACKWOOD CHRONICLE — SCROLL I

In the age before the Great Silence, a brotherhood known as the Star Keepers
erected a ledger of obsidian and bone in the heart of the Blackwood.

Within its pages they inscribed the coordinates of seven celestial bodies —
stars whose alignment, once every thousand years, would open a door
between the world of the living and the realm of pure knowledge.

The first star was called the Seeker's Star, for it appeared only
to those who had humbled themselves enough to look.

The seal upon the ledger bears a triangle — ancient mark of the triad:
mind, spirit, and will — the three gates one must pass before the
deeper mysteries may be approached.

Only when all three gates stand open shall the ledger yield its first truth.
