THE WISDOM STAR — FINAL SCROLL

At the end of every true path lies still water.

Not stagnation, but clarity. The surface of still water
does not distort what it reflects. It shows the world as it is —
not as we wish it to be, nor as we fear it might become.

The Library of Still Waters was built over a natural spring
whose waters have never been disturbed by wind.
The scholars who maintained it took vows of silence,
for they understood that wisdom cannot enter a room full of noise.

Three questions guard the final star.
They are not puzzles to be solved by cleverness.
They are invitations to remember what you already know.

Answer truthfully. The water will judge.

And when the seventh star rises, the Ministry of Nexus is restored —
and the learning that was scattered across seven realms
returns to a single, luminous point.
