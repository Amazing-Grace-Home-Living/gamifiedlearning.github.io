Third Star · Discernment

Blackwood Region

A region of shadow and outline. Nothing announces itself here. The lantern shows only what you are willing to look at.

◈ Blackwood — Enter with a lantern

🔦 Blackwood Landmarks

  • 📒
    The Ledger House A crumbling structure where every door is labeled with what it actually contains, not what was promised. Discernment begins here.
  • 🌑
    The Dim Crossroads Four paths diverge in total darkness. Only one leads forward. The lantern can only light the path you are already facing.
  • 👁️
    The Watching Grove A stand of ancient trees whose roots are shaped like open eyes. Travelers report that assumptions wither and clarity grows here.
  • 📜
    The Archive of Implied Things Scrolls that record truths never spoken aloud — only inferred. To read them is to learn that much of what matters is never announced.
  • 🕯️
    The Keeper's Flame A single candle at the region's edge that never extinguishes. Those who have walked all of Blackwood leave knowing what the flame represents to them — not what they were told.
